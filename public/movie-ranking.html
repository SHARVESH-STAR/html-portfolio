The Best Movies According to Me

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Adios Amigos

A struggling man and a carefree rich stranger form an unlikely friendship during a spontaneous journey that changes both their lives.

Avengers: Endgame

The surviving Avengers travel through time to recover the Infinity Stones and undo the devastation caused by Thanos, leading to an epic final battle to save the universe.

Vikram

A retired black-ops commander secretly returns to action to uncover a dangerous drug syndicate and protect his family in this high-octane action thriller.
